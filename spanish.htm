In the first trimester, we learned:

  • Colors
  • Numbers
  • Family
  • Weather
  • Parts of a house
  • Greetings and goodbyes
  • Seasons
  • Months/seasons
  • Personal information; hola como estas, me llamo, mi nombre, donde vives



In the second trimester, we started to focus more on reading and writing SPanish vocabulary, as well as our comprehension. We also started to work on grammar, like learning about nouns(SUJETOs), adjective(ADJETIVOs), verbs(VERBOs), personal pronouns(yo, tu, el, ella, nosotros, ellos, vosotros, ellas), common and proper, number, and gender. We are now learning about SER and ESTAR



Novels

We read the novel La Casa y la Dentista which is about an evil dentist and discovering the truth about the evil dentist(and all the parents). We learned parts of house and emotions. We learned a lot of new vocabulary in this book as well.


We read the novel Billy y las Botas. It was about a scarf who got lost, found the 2 pink boots, and it was a love story. We learned emotions, seasons, clothes, body parts
