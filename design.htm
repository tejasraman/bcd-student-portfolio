Design
This is a class about building creativity, learning how to set our own goals, as well as learning about and following the design cycle. For each project, first we have to identify the problem and research solutions. Then, we define our success criteria and come up with lots of ideas. After that, we create a solution to the problem. Finally, we test and reflect upon our designs. We keep a detailed record of our design process in our physical and digital process journals.

So far this year, we have created covers for our design journals, 3d printed planters, designed the class sweatshirt, and most recently we have started coding our own video game.
