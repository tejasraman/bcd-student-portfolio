Design
We started off our year in Design by creating a notebook cover thatv reflected our identities with tissue paper. After that, we created 3-D printed self-watering planters using 3D design software. We also created designs for the 8th garde sweatshirts as part of a third mini-unit. We have just started our unit on game design.

I have learned about the Design cycle and how to be informed while designing a product. These skills include how to conduct relevant surveys of user groups, as well as how to use research effectively to design a good product.
