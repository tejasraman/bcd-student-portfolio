Design
In the 2nd trimester students researched on three natural and manmade wonders, one in Colorado, one in the US and one anywhere in the world and found some very interesting places.They also worked on Inventions,Innovations, and discussed about some bad designs. The Kindness poster, MLK poster,and Bookmarks were projects students enjoyed designing. As part of the Infrastructure and Sustainability topic students learned about the importance of Infrastructure and designed bridges out of popsicle sticks, strings, cardboard,tested the strength of the bridge and presented it to the class.
