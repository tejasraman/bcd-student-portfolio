Language &
Literature
We started off our year by writing book reviews on a book that we had read over the summer. After that, we started a short unit on Amplify about role models and concluded the unit with an essay about role models.

We then started our unit on Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. In this unit, we focused mainly on analysis of the text and its characters. We concluded this unit with a reading comprehension assessment (IB 6) and an essay (IB 7). We also did 2 vocabulary tests (IB 8 and IB 7).

We have just started a unit on Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet. We are working on memorizing lines of the Prologue and the First Encounter, as well as analyzing the text and the motives of the characters.
