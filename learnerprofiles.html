IB Learner
Profile
The 10 IB Learner Profile traits are Inquirers Knowledgeable, Thinkers, Communicators, Principled, Open-minded, Caring, Risk-takers, Balanced, and Reflective.


I think that I embody the Inquirer learning trait because I like asking questions and think asking questions is important to truly learning and understanding and I can learn both independently and in a group setting. I also think I also embody the “Thinker” learner trait because I find really thinking about things before doing them is extremely important.


One trait where I can improve is the “Reflective” learning trait. I find it hard to reflect upon my progress and need to think about it for a very long time. I am improving on this and I am already better at this than I was in my 6th grade year. I will continue to improve upon reflecting on my work.
