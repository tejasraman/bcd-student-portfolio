IB Learner
Profile
The 10 IB Learner Profile traits are Inquirer, Knowledgeable, Thinkers, Communicators, Principled, Open-minded, Caring, Risk-takers, Balanced, and Reflective.

I think that I embody the Inquirer learning trait because I believe that asking questions is important to truly learning and understanding and I can learn both independently and in a group setting. I also think I also embody the “Thinker” learner trait because I find that taking time to think about everything I do will almost always lead to a better outcome.

One trait where I can improve is the “Reflective” learning trait. I find it difficult to reflect upon my progress and, as a result, it usually takes me a large amount of time to reflect upon myself. I strive to improve this skill and it has improved vastly since I was in 6th grade.
