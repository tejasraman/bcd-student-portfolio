Art
In 1st trimester visual arts, we drew on and painted skateboard decks with a design of our choosing. We also painted 2 watercolor pieces based on photos we took at Chataqua as part of a class field trip.


In 2nd trimester Media arts we have been learning about the field of video journalism. We have watched many student created newspieces from PBS student Reporting Labs and have been working on the skills needed to create our own piece. Some skills we’ve learned are how to create video sequences, interviewing skills, collaborating with others to develop a script, and using Wevideo editing software to polish and refine our videos.
