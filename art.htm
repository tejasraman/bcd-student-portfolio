In Science, we started off the year by learning about ecosystems, and how one organism being affected allects every organism. We also created endangered species slideshows, and mine was about the Steller Sea Lion.




Then, we started to learn about Newton's laws, and forces in motion. We did an "invisible forces experiment" and I got an IB 5 on the IB B(the data ans experiment procedure), but did badly on the C(conclusion/claim) as i hastily typed it out and did not use my data to support it. This is something I feel like I have improved on and will continue to try and improve on.



Then, we began a unit on energy and all of its different forms, including kinetic and potential energy. At the end of the unit. we did an experiment with energy forms. Mine was about using an electrically charged balloon to roll a ball in different temperatures. This experiment was really enjoyable for me, and I also learned something new - the humidity affected my results.


I also wanted to showcase my science fair project. My project was about whether sound waves could move small objects. I really found the science of sound waves (which I researched after conducting the experiment) quite interesting, and the results themselves surprised me.
