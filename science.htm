Science
This year in Science we are learning about Earth science. We began our year by studying rocks and minerals. Then we began learing about the different layers of the Earth. We also studied Earth's history, as well as how plate tectonics has shaped the Earth's history. We just completed our unit on weather and climate, ending the unit by talking about the devastating impacts of climate change and how it is a massive global problem. I plan to reduce food waste as part of my effort to reduce climate change.
