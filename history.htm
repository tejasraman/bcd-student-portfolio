For my Model UN project, I worked with other students to research the country of Bolivia. We began by learning about the structure and goals of the United Nations. Then, we discussed human rights and the Universal Declaration of Human Rights. We explored our country’s people, customs, lifestyle, government, economy, and demographics. Then we researched news articles to learn about the current events in our country. Last, we identified an issue our country has (or cares about) and wrote a resolution. Our resolution was about improving education and making secondary schools free in Bolivia. My favorite parts of the Model UN project are the resolution telling and learning more about countries. The most challenging part of the Model UN project for me was writing the resolution. Last, I will show you my Model UN website, which I have linked here.
