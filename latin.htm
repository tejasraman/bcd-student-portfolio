In Latin class, we have been learning from the Ecce Romani textbook. We have been learning about Latin grammar, including singular/plural wirds and endings, masculine/feminine words and word endings, and, more recently, the concept of infinitives and complimentary infinitives.


We have also been learning Latin vocabulary, including verbs, nouns, and pronouns, as well as how to tell someone else's name, like "The girl's name is Cornelia", and how to describe what someone else is doing, like "Cornelia is playing".


I got an IB 6 on my Chapter 4 assessment and an IB 6 on my Chapter 5 assessment.
